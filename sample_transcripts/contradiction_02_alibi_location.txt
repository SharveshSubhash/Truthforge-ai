DISTRICT COURT OF SINGAPORE
CRIMINAL CASE NO. DC/CC 88/2025

PUBLIC PROSECUTOR v LEE KAH SENG

Date of Hearing: 22 February 2025
Coram: District Judge Pravin Raj
Counsel for Prosecution: Deputy Public Prosecutor Mr. Aaron Goh Wee Liang
Counsel for Defence: Ms. Jasmine Tan Siew Ping

CASE SUMMARY:
The accused is charged under s.324 of the Penal Code for voluntarily causing
grievous hurt using a glass bottle at Bedok Interchange Hawker Centre, Stall 38
on 5 December 2024 at approximately 8:15pm.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF PW1 (First Prosecution Witness — Mr. Vikram s/o
Balakrishnan, victim)

DPP GOH: Mr. Vikram, what happened to you on the evening of 5 December 2024?
PW1: I was having dinner alone at Bedok Interchange Hawker Centre. I was
sitting at table 14, near the entrance of Stall 38 — the chicken rice stall.
I had just received my food when a man came up from behind me and smashed
a glass bottle on the back of my head. I fell from my chair immediately.
I later needed seven stitches.

DPP GOH: Can you identify the person who attacked you?
PW1: Yes. That man there — Mr. Lee Kah Seng. I know him from the same
block. He has threatened me before about a carpark dispute.

DPP GOH: What time did the attack happen?
PW1: It was 8:17pm. I know because I had just checked my phone for the
time — I was waiting for a message from my wife.

DPP GOH: No further questions.

---

CROSS-EXAMINATION OF PW1

MS. TAN: Mr. Vikram, you said you know Mr. Lee from the same block. Is it
possible you are mistaken about the identity of your attacker, given the
attack was from behind?
PW1: I saw him clearly as I fell. I looked up and I saw his face.

MS. TAN: You had just suffered a blow to the head. Was your vision impaired?
PW1: I was in pain but I saw him clearly before I lost consciousness.

MS. TAN: No further questions.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF PW2 (Second Prosecution Witness — Ms. Chua Li Ling,
eyewitness, seated at adjacent table)

DPP GOH: Ms. Chua, were you at Bedok Interchange Hawker Centre on
5 December 2024?
PW2: Yes. I was at table 12, right next to the victim.

DPP GOH: What did you see?
PW2: I saw a man walk up behind Mr. Vikram very quickly and hit him over
the head with a bottle. The bottle shattered.

DPP GOH: What did the attacker look like?
PW2: He was Chinese, medium build, wearing a blue polo shirt and dark
jeans. He was about 1.70 to 1.75 metres tall. He had a small tattoo
on his left forearm — looked like a dragon.

DPP GOH: Do you see that person in court today?
PW2: Yes. It is the accused, Mr. Lee.

DPP GOH: What time did this occur?
PW2: Around 8:15pm. Maybe 8:15 or 8:20pm. I wasn't watching the time
exactly but it was early evening.

DPP GOH: No further questions.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF PW3 (Third Prosecution Witness — Sergeant Lim Wei
Jie, Singapore Police Force, Bedok Division)

DPP GOH: Sgt Lim, when did you respond to the incident at Bedok Interchange?
PW3: We received the call at 8:22pm and arrived at 8:29pm. The victim was
being attended to by a member of the public. We reviewed CCTV footage
from the hawker centre, which clearly shows a man matching the accused's
description at the scene at 8:16pm.

DPP GOH: Is the CCTV footage in evidence?
PW3: Yes, Exhibit P7. The timestamp on the camera reads 20:16:04 when
the attack occurs.

DPP GOH: No further questions.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF DW1 (First Defence Witness — Mr. Dennis Toh Chin Aik,
colleague of accused)

MS. TAN: Mr. Dennis, where were you on the evening of 5 December 2024?
DW1: I was at Tampines Mall food court with Kah Seng. We had dinner together.

MS. TAN: What time were you with Mr. Lee?
DW1: We met at Tampines MRT at around 7:30pm and walked to Tampines Mall.
We had dinner from about 7:45pm and stayed until about 9:30pm.

MS. TAN: So Mr. Lee was with you the entire time from 7:30pm to 9:30pm?
DW1: Yes, the whole time. He never left.

MS. TAN: No further questions.

---

CROSS-EXAMINATION OF DW1

DPP GOH: Mr. Dennis, Bedok Interchange is approximately 10 kilometres from
Tampines Mall. Do you agree that it is impossible to be at both places
at 8:16pm?
DW1: Yes, that's impossible.

DPP GOH: Yet CCTV shows a man matching the accused's description at Bedok
Interchange at exactly 8:16pm. You are saying Mr. Lee was at Tampines
at that time?
DW1: Yes. He was with me the entire time. The CCTV must be wrong or it
is someone who looks like him.

DPP GOH: Did you and Mr. Lee pay for dinner? Is there a receipt?
DW1: I think I paid by NETS. I might have a transaction record.

DPP GOH: We have obtained Mr. Lee's EZ-Link card records for 5 December 2024.
[Exhibit P9 — EZ-Link travel records showing Mr. Lee tapped out at
Tampines MRT at 18:52 and tapped in at Bedok MRT at 19:58 on
5 December 2024.] Mr. Dennis, Mr. Lee's card shows him arriving at
Bedok at 7:58pm — over an hour before you say he was with you. How do
you explain this?
DW1: I... I don't know. Maybe he had two cards.

DPP GOH: No further questions.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF DW2 (Second Defence Witness — Mr. Lee Kah Seng,
the Accused)

MS. TAN: Mr. Lee, did you attack Mr. Vikram at Bedok Interchange on
5 December 2024?
DW2: No, Your Honour. I was not at Bedok that night. I was having dinner
with my colleague Dennis at Tampines Mall.

MS. TAN: What time were you at Tampines Mall?
DW2: From about 7:45pm to 9:30pm. Dennis can confirm.

---

CROSS-EXAMINATION OF DW2

DPP GOH: Mr. Lee, I am showing you Exhibit P9 — your own EZ-Link records.
These show you tapped in at Bedok MRT at 19:58 on 5 December 2024. This
directly contradicts your evidence that you were at Tampines Mall from
7:45pm. Do you have an explanation?
DW2: I... I must have gone to Bedok first and then to Tampines after.

DPP GOH: You earlier said you met Dennis at Tampines MRT at 7:30pm. But
you tapped in at Bedok MRT at 7:58pm. How could you be at Tampines at
7:30pm and Bedok at 7:58pm?
DW2: Maybe I'm wrong about the time I met Dennis. Maybe it was later.

DPP GOH: Earlier you were certain you met Dennis at 7:30pm. You have now
changed your evidence. Which is it, Mr. Lee?
DW2: I may have been confused about the time.

DPP GOH: No further questions.

COURT: The court records the following contradictions: The accused and DW1
both claim the accused was at Tampines Mall from 7:30–7:45pm. The
accused's own EZ-Link records place him at Bedok MRT at 7:58pm.
The accused has changed his evidence when confronted with the
documentary record.

[HEARING ADJOURNED TO 24 FEBRUARY 2025]
