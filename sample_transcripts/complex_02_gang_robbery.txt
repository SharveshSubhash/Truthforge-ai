HIGH COURT OF SINGAPORE
CRIMINAL CASE NO. HC/CC 19/2025

PUBLIC PROSECUTOR v (1) HAFIZ bin HASSAN (2) RIZAL bin SULAIMAN (3) AMIRUL bin AZMAN

Date of Hearing: 28 March 2025
Coram: Justice Vincent Hoong
Counsel for Prosecution: Senior Deputy Public Prosecutor Mr. Ng Peng Hong and DPP Ms. Teo Lay Khim
Counsel for First Accused: Mr. Hassan Iqbal SC
Counsel for Second Accused: Mr. Andrew Phang
Counsel for Third Accused: Ms. Liew Shu Ting

CASE SUMMARY:
Three accused persons face charges under s.395 of the Penal Code (gang
robbery) arising from an armed robbery at a money changer at Mustafa Centre,
Serangoon Road, on 14 December 2024. The three men are alleged to have acted
in concert. All three were arrested within 72 hours. Their accounts of events
differ significantly from each other.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF PW1 (First Prosecution Witness — Mr. Ng Ah Kow,
money changer operator, Mustafa Centre Unit #02-140)

SDPP NG: Mr. Ng, describe what happened on 14 December 2024.
PW1: At about 2:20pm, three men came to my counter. Two came to the
front — one of them pushed a note through the grille saying "give all
your $50 notes." The third man went to the side and I saw him tamper
with my CCTV cable — I found later the cable had been cut. The two
in front had their faces partially covered with surgical masks. One
had a short screwdriver which he waved at me. I was scared and pushed
my tray of $50 notes through — $4,200 worth. They then ran out together.

SDPP NG: How long were you in contact with them?
PW1: Maybe 2 to 3 minutes at the counter.

SDPP NG: No further questions.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF PW2 (Second Prosecution Witness — Senior Staff
Sergeant Mohamed Yusof bin Kassim, CNB, arresting officer for A3)

SDPP NG: Staff Sergeant, how was the third accused arrested?
PW2: On 16 December 2024, acting on information, I arrested the third
accused, Mr. Amirul bin Azman, at his residence in Ang Mo Kio. In his
bedroom we found $1,400 in $50 notes in a shoebox. He was cautioned
and said: "I was there but I only cut the wire. Hafiz and Rizal grabbed
the money. I didn't take anything."

SDPP NG: No further questions.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF DW1 (First Accused — Mr. Hafiz bin Hassan)

MR. IQBAL SC: Mr. Hafiz, were you at Mustafa Centre on 14 December 2024?
DW1: Yes, I was at Mustafa Centre, but I was shopping. I went there
alone. I knew Rizal was also going to be in the area that day but I
did not plan any robbery with him.

MR. IQBAL SC: Did you approach the money changer?
DW1: I walked past the money changer area. I did not go to any counter.

MR. IQBAL SC: Do you know the third accused, Amirul?
DW1: I know who he is. We are acquaintances from the neighbourhood.
I did not go to Mustafa with him.

MR. IQBAL SC: No further questions.

---

CROSS-EXAMINATION OF DW1

SDPP NG: Mr. Hafiz, cell phone location data, Exhibit P14, places your
phone within 50 metres of the Mustafa Centre money changer area at 2:18pm
on 14 December 2024. Are you saying this is coincidence?
DW1: I was in the mall. The phone was with me.

SDPP NG: Rizal's phone was also in the same location at the same time.
And Amirul's. Three coincidences?
DW1: I can't explain what Rizal or Amirul were doing.

SDPP NG: Exhibit P6 is CCTV from Mustafa Centre Level 2. It shows three
men matching the description of the accused entering the money changer
area together at 2:18pm. You say you were not at the counter. The figure
at the CCTV image — can you confirm whether that is you?
DW1: I am not saying anything further.

SDPP NG: No further questions.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF DW2 (Second Accused — Mr. Rizal bin Sulaiman)

MR. PHANG: Mr. Rizal, what happened on 14 December 2024?
DW2: I was at Mustafa to buy a watch. I ran into Hafiz there. We walked
around together. I did not take part in any robbery. If something
happened at the money changer, it was Hafiz's idea and I was not involved.

MR. PHANG: Did you approach the money changer?
DW2: I stood nearby while Hafiz and Amirul went to the counter. I did
not know what they were doing.

MR. PHANG: No further questions.

---

CROSS-EXAMINATION OF DW2

SDPP NG: Mr. Rizal, you say you "stood nearby" and "did not know" what
was happening. Yet Exhibit P6 shows the figure matching your description
leaning over the counter alongside the figure matching Hafiz. How do
you explain this?
DW2: I may have leaned over to look at the exchange rates.

SDPP NG: Did you wear a surgical mask into the mall?
DW2: I sometimes wear a mask.

SDPP NG: Mr. Amirul's statement to police, Exhibit P9, says: "Rizal was
the one with the screwdriver. He told me and Hafiz what to do." Now you
say you knew nothing. Whose account is correct?
DW2: Amirul is lying to protect himself.

SDPP NG: No further questions.

COURT: The court notes the following conflicting accounts:
- A1 Hafiz: Claims he was nearby but not at the counter, alone. Does
  not deny being in the vicinity.
- A2 Rizal: Claims he was present but passive; now implicates A1 and A3
  as the primary actors.
- A3 Amirul: Admitted his presence and role (CCTV wire cutting) but
  implicates A2 (Rizal) as having the screwdriver and directing the robbery.
- The prosecution's CCTV and cell data are consistent for all three accuseds
  being present and acting in coordination.
- The three accused persons' accounts are mutually inconsistent on the
  specific roles each played, though all are consistent that all three
  were present.
