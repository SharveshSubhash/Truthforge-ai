HIGH COURT OF SINGAPORE
CRIMINAL CASE NO. HC/CC 7/2025

PUBLIC PROSECUTOR v AZHAR BIN MOHD YUNUS

Date of Hearing: 27 January 2025
Coram: Justice Audrey Lim Li Cheng
Counsel for Prosecution: Deputy Public Prosecutor Mr. Henry Choo Pak Yew
Counsel for Defence: Ms. Parvathi d/o Sithamparam

CASE SUMMARY:
The accused is charged under s.8(a) of the Misuse of Drugs Act (MDA) for
possession of a controlled drug — specifically, not less than 14.76 grams of
methamphetamine — found during a CNB raid at Block 712 Jurong West Street 71,
#10-154, on 8 October 2024 at 2:15am. A further charge under s.9 MDA for
possession of drug-related utensils is joined to the same hearing.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF PW1 (First Prosecution Witness — Station Inspector
Noor Hafizuddin bin Othman, Central Narcotics Bureau)

DPP CHOO: SI Noor, were you the investigating officer for this case?
PW1: Yes, I was the officer-in-charge of the operation on 8 October 2024.

DPP CHOO: Can you describe the operation?
PW1: We received intelligence that the accused was in possession of
controlled drugs at his flat. At 2:10am, a team of four CNB officers
including myself attended at Block 712 Jurong West Street 71, #10-154.
We knocked and the accused opened the door. We identified ourselves
and gained entry. We showed the accused the search warrant.

DPP CHOO: What was found during the search?
PW1: In the master bedroom, inside a blue Adidas shoebox on the wardrobe
shelf, we found: one clear plastic packet containing crystalline
substance, later confirmed as methamphetamine, and one improvised
pipe made from a glass bottle, consistent with drug consumption.
The accused was the sole occupant of the flat at the time of the raid.

DPP CHOO: Was the accused cooperative?
PW1: Yes. He did not resist and made no attempt to flee or destroy evidence.

DPP CHOO: No further questions.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF PW2 (Second Prosecution Witness — Sergeant Zul Hafri
bin Azman, CNB officer present at the scene)

DPP CHOO: Sgt Zul, were you present at the search on 8 October 2024?
PW2: Yes, I was one of the four officers who conducted the search.

DPP CHOO: Where exactly was the shoebox found?
PW2: SI Noor located it on the top shelf of the built-in wardrobe in the
master bedroom — the bedroom on the left side of the flat. It was at
the back of the shelf, behind some folded clothing.

DPP CHOO: At what time was the shoebox found?
PW2: At approximately 2:22am, about 12 minutes after we entered the flat.

DPP CHOO: No further questions.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF PW3 (Third Prosecution Witness — Dr. Ng Swee Peng,
HSA Analyst, Health Sciences Authority)

DPP CHOO: Dr. Ng, did you analyse the substance found in the plastic packet?
PW3: Yes. The exhibit was submitted to me on 9 October 2024. I conducted
a full chemical analysis. The crystalline substance contained in the
packet was confirmed to be methamphetamine. The net weight of the
substance was 14.76 grams. I issued HSA Certificate No. AT-2024-11038,
which is Exhibit P6.

DPP CHOO: Is there any doubt as to the nature of the substance?
PW3: None. The analysis was conclusive.

DPP CHOO: No further questions.

---

CROSS-EXAMINATION OF PW3

MS. PARVATHI: Dr. Ng, is it possible that two different samples could
produce similar results if cross-contamination occurred at the lab?
PW3: Our procedures include strict chain of custody protocols. The exhibit
was sealed when received and the seal was intact. Each sample is handled
in isolation. Cross-contamination is not a realistic concern with the
protocols in place.

MS. PARVATHI: No further questions.

---

EXAMINATION-IN-CHIEF OF DW1 (The Accused — Mr. Azhar bin Mohd Yunus)

MS. PARVATHI: Mr. Azhar, did the shoebox belong to you?
DW1: Yes, it is my shoebox.

MS. PARVATHI: Did you know the drugs were inside it?
DW1: No. I did not put them there. I had lent my key to a friend —
I know him as "Boy" — about two weeks before the raid. He returned
the key but I did not know he had gone into my room.

MS. PARVATHI: Did "Boy" have access to your bedroom wardrobe?
DW1: He must have. I did not know he had put anything in my room.

---

CROSS-EXAMINATION OF DW1

DPP CHOO: Mr. Azhar, can you give us "Boy's" full name, identification
number, or contact details so we can investigate this claim?
DW1: I only know him as "Boy." I met him through a mutual friend. I don't
have his number anymore.

DPP CHOO: You lent your flat key to someone whose full name you don't know?
DW1: We were friendly at the time.

DPP CHOO: The drugs were found behind folded clothing inside your personal
shoebox on a high shelf. This is not a casual hiding place. Does "Boy"
know where you keep your personal shoeboxes?
DW1: I don't know how he found it.

DPP CHOO: CNB records show this is your third interaction with law
enforcement on drug-related matters. Were the previous two incidents
also due to a mysterious friend planting drugs in your home?
DW1: Those were different.

DPP CHOO: No further questions.

COURT: All prosecution witnesses are consistent on all material facts
regarding the time of the raid (2:10–2:15am), the location of the
drugs (master bedroom wardrobe, blue Adidas shoebox), the identity
of the sole occupant, and the confirmed weight and nature of the
substance (14.76g methamphetamine). The accounts of PW1, PW2 and
PW3 do not contradict each other in any material respect.

[HEARING ADJOURNED FOR FURTHER SUBMISSIONS ON 29 JANUARY 2025]
